Stage Fright is a rock/metal band based in Cincinatti, OH. The band was formed in 2023 by friends Dallas "Texas" Wraith[lead guitar/vocals], Askey Hourse[rhythm guitar/vocals], Fernandes Mulesteele[bass], and Rory Thunderknox[drums] over their shared enjoyment for music. The band's first single, "Over The Under," was released in 2024 and has since gained over 3 million streams across platforms. The band is currently on tour promoting their debut album, "The Midnight Echoes," which was released in January 2025. The band's music is a blend of heavy metal, metalcore, and hardcore punk, with influences from bands such as Killswitch Engage, 36 Crazyfists, and August Burns Red. Stage Fright is known for their high-energy local shows and powerful lyrics, which often deal with themes of mental health, addiction, and personal struggle.
