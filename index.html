Dallas "Texas" Wraith

Dallas "Texas" Wraith is the lead guitarist and vocalist of Stage Fright. Known for his electrifying stage presence and technical prowess, Dallas brings a unique blend of intensity and emotion to the band's performances. His influences range from classic rock legends to modern metal icons, and his playing style is characterized by powerful riffs, intricate solos, and soulful melodies. Offstage, Dallas is a dedicated musician who spends countless hours perfecting his craft and writing new material. His passion for music is matched only by his commitment to his bandmates and fans.


Askey Hourse

Askey Hourse is the rhythm guitarist and backing vocalist of Stage Fright. With a background in classical guitar and a love for heavy metal, Askey brings a unique and versatile sound to the band. His rhythm playing is tight and precise, providing a solid foundation for the band's complex arrangements. Askey's vocal harmonies add depth and richness to the band's sound, complementing Dallas's lead vocals perfectly. Offstage, Askey is known for his laid-back personality and his dedication to his craft. He spends his free time writing new music, experimenting with different sounds, and honing his skills as both a guitarist and a vocalist.


Fernandes Mulesteele

Fernandes Mulesteele is the bassist of Stage Fright. With a background in jazz and classical music, Fernandes brings a unique and sophisticated sound to the band's heavy metal roots. His bass lines are both melodic and powerful, providing a solid backbone to the band's music. Fernandes is known for his technical skill and his ability to seamlessly blend different musical styles. Offstage, Fernandes is a dedicated musician who spends his time practicing, composing, and exploring new musical genres. His passion for music and his commitment to his bandmates make him an invaluable member of Stage Fright.


Rory Thunderknox

Rory Thunderknox is the drummer of Stage Fright. With a background in both jazz and metal drumming, Rory brings a dynamic and powerful energy to the band's rhythm section. His drumming is characterized by intricate patterns, explosive fills, and a relentless drive that propels the band's music forward. Rory's versatility and technical skill make him a standout performer, both in the studio and on stage. Offstage, Rory is a dedicated musician who spends his time practicing, composing, and exploring new drumming techniques. His passion for music and his commitment to his bandmates make him an essential part of Stage Fright.


Stage Fright is a rock/metal band based in Cincinatti, OH. The band was formed in 2023 by friends Dallas "Texas" Wraith[lead guitar/vocals], Askey Hourse[rhythm guitar/vocals], Fernandes Mulesteele[bass], and Rory Thunderknox[drums] over their shared enjoyment for music. The band's first single, "Over The Under," was released in 2024 and has since gained over 3 million streams across platforms. The band is currently on tour promoting their debut album, "The Midnight Echoes," which was released in January 2025. The band's music is a blend of heavy metal, metalcore, and hardcore punk, with influences from bands such as Killswitch Engage, 36 Crazyfists, and August Burns Red. Stage Fright is known for their high-energy local shows and powerful lyrics, which often deal with themes of mental health, addiction, and personal struggle.
